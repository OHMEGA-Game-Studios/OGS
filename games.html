The Ruined Manifest

Overview:

The Ruined Manifest, set in a captivating natural expanse. The Thrashaw Hills. The Ruined Manifest is a survival horror game set in the Cascade Mountains of Washington State. The game will tell a story about a girl's life in a forest that she has gotten lost in. She will learn about the forest and its history. The game will be released for PC, MAC, and Linux.

Release Info:

As of 2024 however the game will be in the pre-release stage and available for PC and MAC. Though we are currently researching linux support. There will be a launcher for this game later. This launcher will first come out as a PC and MAC launcher and later in October of 2025 we will have a linux launcher for the game.
